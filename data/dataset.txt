## Dataset
The project uses a flight dataset containing historical flight information, including various features such as airline, origin, destination, scheduled departure time, and others. You can download the dataset from [here]( https://drive.google.com/file/d/1iTU4-4Z1rxwRspi8AUY87PAbnndce4u1/view?usp=sharing) and place it in the `data/` directory.
